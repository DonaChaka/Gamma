psychology What Gamma Does

Gamma is a visual assistant designed to automate workflows in a desktop environment. It can also act as a study assistant, extracting screen contents and equations in an organized format.

Screen Sharing Enabled

play_circle Current Functionality

  • Explain what’s happening on your screen
  • Extract equations, text, and visuals in LaTeX format
  • Copy responses directly into your preferred app

memory Technology Used

Gamma uses lightweight Visual Language Models (VLMs) in the range of 1B–8B parameters. This ensures smooth performance on edge devices and low‑powered hardware.

rocket_launch Future Developments

  • GUI navigation and automation
  • Integration with TexWorks, Word, PowerPoint, and browsers
  • Seamless app interaction for productivity
